About The Techno-Builders

The Techno-Builders (TTB), the official student publication of the University pf Southern Mindanao-Kidapawan City Campus, is an autonomous, non-organizational, or non-academic guild composed of student writers and artists. Training, developing, and exposing journalists in various fields such as Editorial, News, Feature and Literary Writing, Cartooning and Visual Arts, Photography, Videography, Graphic Designing, and Layouting. TTB, being directly under the administration, is also assessed for International Organization for Standardization (ISO) certifications and recertifications.

Constitution and Bylaws

Our organization is guided by a constitution that ensures fairness, transparency, and a commitment to our mission. It outlines our structure, membership, and operational procedures.
